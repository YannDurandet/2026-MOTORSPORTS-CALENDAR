FAQ
What time zone are the race times in?
All times are displayed in your system time. By default, the master calendar is built using Paris (CET/CEST) timezone.
When does the 2026 Formula 1 season start?
The 2026 F1 season kicks off with the Australian Grand Prix on March 8, 2026.
Which series are included in this tracker?
We track 21 major global and regional championships including Formula 1, WEC, IMSA, IndyCar, WRC, MotoGP, WorldSBK, NASCAR, and Supercars.
How do the filters work?
You can toggle specific series on and off using the buttons at the top of the calendar. The dashboard countdowns will automatically update to reflect your active filters.
